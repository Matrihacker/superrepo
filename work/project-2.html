SINEWAVE

A instruments that you can play in the air
Project Overview
SINEWAVE is a synthesizer intrument that uses the IR distance sensor to detecting distance and generating the sound
Inspiration
I always want to make a instrucment that can release our hand from the surface. I want to play the music through the air. After I saw the Theremin, I start to think about using the sensor to detect the distance and tranfer it into soundwave
